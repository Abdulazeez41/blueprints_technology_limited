Engagement Process

Understanding our typical engagement flow

Initial Consultation

We begin with a structured discovery session to understand your business objectives, technical requirements, and organizational context. This typically involves:

  • Requirements documentation review
  • Technical architecture discussion
  • Timeline and budget framework alignment
  • Team and governance structure review

Initial consultations are conducted without obligation and serve as mutual evaluation opportunities.

Proposal & Agreement

Following discovery, we provide a detailed proposal including:

  • Scope of work and deliverables
  • Technical approach and architecture
  • Timeline and milestones
  • Team composition and expertise
  • Investment structure
  • Success metrics and acceptance criteria

We structure agreements for transparency and mutual accountability.
